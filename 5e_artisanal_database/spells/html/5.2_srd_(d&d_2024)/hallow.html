Hallow

  • Level: 5 Abjuration
  • Classes: Cleric
  • Casting Time: hour
  • Range: Touch
  • Components: V, S, M (incense worth 1,000+ GP, which the spell consumes)
  • Duration: until dispelled
  • Source: 5.2 SRD (D&D 2024)
