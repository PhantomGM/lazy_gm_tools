
# Horseshoes of Speed

* **Rarity:** rare
* **Type:** Wondrous item
* **Attunement:** No
* **Source:** 5.1 SRD (D&D 2014)


These iron horseshoes come in a set of four. While all four shoes are affixed to the hooves of a horse or similar creature, they increase the creature's walking speed by 30 feet.
