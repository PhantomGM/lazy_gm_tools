Dancing Shoes

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up A5E

These shoes feature a low heel, but otherwise shape to the wearer’s foot. While wearing these shoes, you gain an expertise die on checks that relate to dancing.

If the shoes are separated, you may attune to one. When attuned to in this way, you know the direction of its location and if another creature has attuned to it. Once the shoes are reunited again, the effect ends.
