Potion of Growth

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Potion
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up A5E

A golden acorn floating in this translucent liquid grows and retracts roots every few seconds. After drinking this potion, for the next 1d4 hours your size is doubled (as the __enlarge/reduce_ spell but without need for concentration ).
