Brazier of Commanding Fire Elementals

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up A5E

While fire burns in this 5 pound brass brazier, you can use an action to summon a fire elemental as if you had cast the __conjure elemental_ spell. Once used, you must wait until the next dawn to use it again.
