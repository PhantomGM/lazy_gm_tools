Wand of the War Mage +1

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wand
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up A5E

You must be a spellcaster to attune to this wand. While holding this wand, you gain a bonus to spell attack rolls (uncommon +1), and you ignore half cover when making spell attacks.
