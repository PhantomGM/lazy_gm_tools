Ring of Animal Influence

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Ring
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up A5E

While wearing this ring, you can expend 1 of its 3 charges to cast one of the following spells (save DC 13): animal friendship , fear targeting only beasts that have an Intelligence of 3 or less, or speak with animals . The ring regains 1d3 expended charges at dawn.
