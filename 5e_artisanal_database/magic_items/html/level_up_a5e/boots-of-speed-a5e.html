Boots of Speed

  • Rarity: Rare
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up A5E

While wearing these boots, you can use a bonus action to click the heels together. You double your base Speed, and opportunity attacks made against you have disadvantage . You can end the effect as a bonus action.

Once the boots have been used in this way for a total of 10 minutes (each use expends a minimum of 1 minute), they cease to function until you finish a long rest .
