Talisman of the Sphere

  • Rarity: Legendary
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up A5E

When you hold this talisman while making an Intelligence (Arcana) check to control a __sphere of annihilation_ add your proficiency bonus twice. If you are in control of a sphere of annihilation at the start of your turn, you can use an action to move the sphere 10 feet plus a number of additional feet equal to 10 × your Intelligence modifier.
