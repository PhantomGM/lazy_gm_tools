Sword of the Serpent

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Weapon
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up A5E

You gain a +2 bonus to attack and damage rolls made with this magic sword. When you hit with this weapon, you deal an extra 1d6 poison damage. The sword has 3 charges and regains all expended charges at dawn.

While wielding the sword, you can use an action to expend 1 charge and cast __polymorph_ on yourself, transforming into a giant poisonous snake . While in this form, you retain your Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma scores.
