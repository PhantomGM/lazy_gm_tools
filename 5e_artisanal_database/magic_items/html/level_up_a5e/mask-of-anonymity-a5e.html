Mask of Anonymity

  • Rarity: Common
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up A5E

This masquerade mask covers the top half of your face in an elegant fan of papier-mache and paint, rendering you anonymous. A creature may make an Insight check opposed by your Deception to determine your identity, but does so at disadvantage , even through a magical_/_ sensor.
