Water Charm

  • Rarity: Uncommon
  • Type: Wondrous Item
  • Attunement: No
  • Source: Level Up A5E

While wearing this charm you gain a swim speed equal to your Speed, or you can break the charm to release its power, destroying it to activate one of the following effects.

  • Breathe: Cast water breathing .
  • Cure: Cast __cure wounds_ at 3rd-level (+3 spellcasting ability modifier).
  • Numb: Cast sleet storm (spell save DC 15).

Curse. Releasing the charm’s power attracts the attention of a marid or water elemental who seeks you out to recruit you for a task.
