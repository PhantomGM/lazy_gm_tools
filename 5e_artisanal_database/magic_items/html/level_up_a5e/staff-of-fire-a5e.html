Staff of Fire

  • Rarity: Very Rare
  • Type: Staff
  • Attunement: Yes
  • Source: Level Up A5E

While holding this staff you gain resistance to fire damage, and you can use an action to expend charges to cast one of the following spells, using your spell save DC: __burning hands_ (1 charge), __fireball_ (3 charges), wall of fire (4 charges).

The staff has 10 charges and regains 1d6+4 expended charges each dawn. When the last charge is expended, roll a d20. On a 1, the staff crumbles to ash and is destroyed.
